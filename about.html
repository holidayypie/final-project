About
The most important thing to me for me to stay in my good energy is to be outside! I think we can find peace and healing just by getting some fresh air and some sun. One of my favorite outdoor activities is to go rafting. I love anything that has to do with being out in the water. Its also a great activity to enjoy as a family.
Another thing i like to do to help get my mind into a more positive state is to go swimming. My personal preference is to swim in natural waters, like oceans, rivers and lakes. I feel more connected to earth when im swimming in the "Earth's water." My favorite swimming spot in California is a place called Coffee Camp in Springville. Its a river with natural pools to swim inside of the Sequoia National Park forest.
